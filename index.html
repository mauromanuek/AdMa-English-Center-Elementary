📚 Teacher's Guide

General Tips for Online Teaching

  • Visuals are Key: Use realia, flashcards, and screen sharing.
  • Encourage Speaking: Create a safe and fun environment. Focus on fluency first.
  • Movement Breaks: Incorporate short physical activities (e.g., "Simon Says").

Unit-Specific Tips

Unit 1: Speaking Mission - Encourage students to use gestures and facial expressions.

Unit 13: "Emotion Charades" is excellent for non-verbal communication and vocabulary review.
